In this page I will cover the mod 'Mixedup'. A mod that includes multiple additions with no special reason. I created the mod with the intention of learning minecraft modding and I believe I have made good progress with the mod that I can share it

On use(right-click) applies frozen status effect to radius of 10 block. Has limited use but it is not available to craft ATM.
On hit(left-click) applies internal burn status effect to the entity for 60 seconds. Has unlimited durability but it is not available to craft ATM.
